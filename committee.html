President

Ceiron Crowley Jones - Chiron

Ceiron who goes by the DJ name Chiron is about to undertake his second year as President of the society. Ceiron has taken the society from its roots to one of the best societies the university has to offer. He has set up the DJ school which has had two successful groups of graduates and now run an advanced class. His hard work has enabled the society to gain more gigs through events and through the univeristy itself, even having members playing at the esteemed Summer Ball.



Vice President

Luke Ebbens - EBBENS

Luke playing as EBBENS has made massive strides in the Drum and Bass community. In his second year on the committee of the society serving as treasurer last year. Working closely with both Riddim Records and Dazed showing ravers just how he can mix. Excited to see what Luke brings to the society for the coming year.



Social Secretary

Alex Booth - Boov


Alex Booth as known as Boov has taken the role of social secretary this year, after a successful year of socials we are hoping Alex can provide us with some top quality nights out. Alex is no stranger behind the decks having played for Sink and Concrete Junglists, and even playing at Swansea's Summer ball



Events Manager

Chris Elia - Prophet


Chris is enjoying his second year on the committe, having had the role of social sec last year, the role was split into two this year, being social sec and events manager. Chris will be in charge of sorting out all the events done through the DJ society. Like the rest of the committee, Chris is comfortable behind the decks having mixed with some of the best the DnB scene has to offer.


Treasurer

James Graham - Jammez


James like many of the other guys on the committee is in his second year of the committee bringing it into the society it is today. As treasurer James will handle all things money within the society. James is also quite the producer with a 6 track EP currently in the works.


Social Media Managers

Kris Bajaj and James Evans - KristOfA and Six7




Both Kris and James are new to the committee this year having both graduated from our DJ School. Both have a keen eye for social media and are hoping to push the society further through the use of social media. All our socials are run through these two. Both have accomplished a lot in a short amount of time of being part of the society.


House Music Manager

Dan Taylor - Duzzy T


Unfortunately everyone on the committee so far is a keen fan for the harder and faster styles of music. So we have someone especially looking after our DJs who are much more inclined to the house, tech and disco side of music. Dan known as Duzzy T has accomplished a fair bit as part of the society including playing Summer Ball, teaching the DJ school and even building this website.
