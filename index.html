Swansea Uni DJ Society

A society built around our love for music and the art of DJing.

Whether you have never touched a set of decks before or you are a mixing professional, there is a place for you in the society. We offer a 6 week beginners DJ school teaching the basics of beat matching and transitioning from one track to another. All leading to your own night playing to Swansea's finest students. For the more experienced DJs we offer an advanced course and even have some of members offer music production lessons. Our more experienced DJs even get to play with some of the biggest names in the music scene today.

Endorsements

A few of the events and clubs that regularly use our DJs.

Bassline

Bassline provides Swansea with some of the biggest and best names in Bassline and DnB music. Having brought names like Andy C, Wilkinson, Shy FX, Holy Goof and many more! Many of our DJs have played alongside these names including our President handing over the decks to Andy C.

Cure

Cure, the sister to Bassline brings the best of House and Tech to Swansea. Bringing the likes of Denis Sulta and Mall Grab, with many more acts lined up.

Ents

Ents is Swansea University's events company. Run by the Student Union, Ents have brought big names to Swansea including High Contrast, David Rodigan, Dimension and Calyx and Teebee

Sin City

Sin City Swansea hold some of the best events Swansea has to offer. Many acts who play in Swansea play in Sin.

Bambu

Bambu, in the heart of Wind Street Swansea hosts our Monday night residency. Bermuda, where our DJs bring the newest and best tracks in House and Tech.

Hangar 18

Hangar 18 has one of the best sound systems in Swansea. Partnered with the DJ Society, Hangar 18 brings Propane a residency aimed to bring the biggest tracks in House and Techno.

Riddim Records

Riddim and Riddim Records are an independent events brand and record label based in Cardiff and founded on all things drum and bass. Riddim have worked closely with members in the DJ Society.

Helix

Helix events are a combination of raw talented artists looking for a chance to perform to a live crowd in a nightclub venue alongside headline acts, so that they can walk away having gained an experience from it. Like Riddim Helix has members working closely within the society.

Dazed

Dazed are an events company based out of Bristol but are regularly bringing some of the top names in DnB to Swansea. With some of our members being resident DJs for Dazed events.
