Our Aim

Split any track into stems, in browser, quickly and painlessly.


Our main goal is to seamlessly allow the general public to pick the lead vocals, bass, piano and more from ANY track. We're also working on allowing the public to train our model with us, giving you access to upload a full track and up to 5 stems for our training model to feast on. Every song and stem our model listens to will increase it's accuracy, meaning eventually we should be able to produce AI split stems of extremely high quality.


Our machine learning algorithm is powered by the latest software in AI driven splitting thanks to Spleeter. We currently have the ability to split into 2 stems, 4 stems and 5 stems each taking longer than the last. In the future we our plans are to allow you to split many songs at once, each of a different format and theoretically infinite in size and length.


Acceptable File formats:


  • mp3
  • mpeg
  • wav
  • flac
  • ogg
  • m4a
  • wma


    • Instructions


    1. 1. Upload any track below to our secure servers, as long as it's a format in our list, we'll split it!

    2. 2. Click upload. If you're worried about copyright or dirty dub traders we have you covered, all uploaded tracks are stored temporarily for the duration of the split. We do not store tunes unless you specifically ask us to.

    3. 3. Keep an eye on the progress bar, and download your brand new stems when they're ready.
